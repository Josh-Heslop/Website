Stacks
Stacks are lists that work on a last in first out (LIFO) basis meaning that if you were to look at the top object in the list it would be the most reacent thing put in there.

Queues
Queues work on a FIFO (First In First Out) basis, this means that if you were to look at the top object in the list it would be the first thing that was put in the list.
